PRODUCT INTRODUCTION

Heytea has always adhered to high quality requirements for its products.

What Heytea wants to share with customers is the authentic taste of the tea. In order to bring the customers high-quality tea, our R&D team pay much attention to product research and development, adhere to the independent product research . The professional laboratory is set up in Shenzhen headquarters, dedicated to tea drink product conception, formulation research and sample manufacture.

The launch of fruit tea series aims to provide customers a wonderful taste of fruit mixing with tea.

Select high-quality tea as the base of fruit tea, and season it with natural sugar, bringing a refreshing and rich taste.

As the seasons rotate, each season has its unique temperament and product. In order to present the season's freshest flavor, Heytea launches season limited series which only use the fruit in season. The cheese strawberry, cheese mango and cheese honey-dew are popular among consumers.

MENU

ORIGINAL CHEESE TEA Combining salt cheese with fresh tea in an ingenious way, HEYTEA original cheese tea is very popular among the customers.
The cheese is imported from New Zealand, the creamy taste matching up with the fragrance of selected tea, which creates a wondeful feeling.
FRESH FRUIT TEA Introducing the fresh fruit tea which brings a refreshing taste of the mix between tea and fruits.
