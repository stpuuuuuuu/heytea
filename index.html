ABOUT US

HEYTEA originated from an instant inspiration of mixing cheese with tea. In 2012, with this spark of inspiration, we opened a small tea shop on a lane called Jiangbianli in Jiangmen City of Guangdong Province. We've come a long way, starting from a 20 mi store to our outlets across Asia today. As the creator of the original Cheezo Tea, HEYTEA sources the finest tea globally, hoping to breathe new life into the traditional tea culture.
